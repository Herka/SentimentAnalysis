RoberEbert_Parser.py
collect movie/game reviews and save them in .txt files that include the rating the subject discussed in the review was given.

"GS8 - Rating 85.txt" means its the 8th collected Gamestar review and the reviewed game got an 85% rating.


The different classifiers use those txts and others (like Tweets collected in a .txt file) to train the NLTK Classifier.
The "Moviereview_Corpus_Classifier.py" is using the Movie Review Corpus that is included in NLTK.
